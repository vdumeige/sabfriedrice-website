About Us

At Sab Fried Rice, we're passionate about bringing authentic Thai flavors to Fort Smith, Arkansas. Our family-owned restaurant combines traditional recipes passed down through generations with locally sourced ingredients to create memorable dining experiences. Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to sharing the vibrant tastes of Thailand while providing warm hospitality that makes every guest feel like family.
