Our featured dishes

Savor the Flavors of Thailand, Crafted in the Heart of Fort Smith.

Green Curry
$11.99

A vibrant symphony of tender vegetables and aromatic herbs, simmered in a rich, creamy green curry sauce that balances spice, sweetness, and the fresh essence of Thailand.

Sab Sampler
$12.99

A vibrant culinary journey showcasing the rich tapestry of Thai flavors – crisp, golden delights and delicate morsels artfully arranged, accompanied by an array of signature dipping sauces that dance between sweet, tangy, and bold.

Chicken Larb
$12.99

Chicken Larb - A vibrant minced chicken salad infused with fresh Thai herbs, lemongrass, and mint leaves, enhanced with a hint of chili and zesty lime dressing. Served with crisp cucumber slices for a refreshing crunch. This iconic northeastern Thai dish delivers a perfect balance of bright flavors and light, satisfying texture.

Tom Yum Combination Seafood Soup
$15.99

A steaming hot pot of Thailand's famous fragrant soup, brimming with succulent shrimp, tender squid, and delicate fish, all swimming in a lemongrass-infused broth. The perfect balance of sour, spicy, and aromatic flavors comes from fresh lime juice, galangal, and kaffir lime leaves, while mushrooms and fresh herbs add depth and complexity. Served in a traditional hot pot to maintain its temperature and allow the flavors to continue developing as you enjoy this invigorating Thai classic.

Nam Khao
$12.99

Crispy fried rice tossed with savory pork, fresh herbs, and aromatic spices, creating a perfect balance of textures and flavors. Brightened with lime juice and garnished with mint leaves and dried chilies, this northern Thai specialty is served with fresh lettuce leaves for wrapping, offering a delightful contrast of warm, crunchy filling and cool, crisp greens.

Yum Kai
$12.99

Succulent grilled chicken tossed with crisp vegetables, fresh herbs, and sliced onions in a vibrant Thai-style dressing. The perfect balance of sweet, sour, and spicy flavors comes from lime juice, fish sauce, and chili, while fresh cilantro adds an aromatic finish. Served with colorful purple cabbage and carrots for added crunch and visual appeal, this refreshing salad delivers the authentic taste of Thailand in every bite.
