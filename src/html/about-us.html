Behind the scenes

Every dish at Sab Fried Rice tells a story of tradition, passion, and the journey that brought authentic Thai cuisine to the heart of Fort Smith.

From humble beginnings in a small kitchen in Bangkok to becoming Fort Smith's beloved Thai destination, our culinary journey spans generations and continents.

Our kitchen is where magic happens daily. Each morning begins with the preparation of fresh ingredients and handcrafted sauces that form the foundation of our menu. Our chefs, trained in traditional Thai cooking methods, carefully balance the four fundamental Thai flavors – sweet, sour, salty, and spicy – to create dishes that transport you straight to the streets of Thailand. We're committed to preserving authenticity while embracing the local Arkansas community that has welcomed us with open arms.
